# Overview

## Project Goals

- Automate TV and movie acquisition
- Provide a clean request interface for users
- Enforce VPN-only torrent traffic
- Keep management tools stable and LAN-accessible
- Document real-world issues and solutions

## Why This Matters

Many media setups break due to:
- VPN misuse
- Cloudflare-blocked indexers
- Overcomplicated networking

This homelab focuses on **simplicity, stability, and security**.
